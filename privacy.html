Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2025

1. Introduction

HalalSnap AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Camera Data: We access your device's camera only to scan barcodes, product ingredients, and menus. Images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers unless you explicitly upload them to the community database.
  • Location Data: We use your location to find nearby Halal restaurants and mosques. This data is not tracked historically.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you use the app to improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to provide the Halal scanning service, improve AI accuracy, and verify community reports.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Gemini API for image analysis. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@halalsnap.ai.
